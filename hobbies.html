My top 5 Hobbies

  1. Open Source Contribution:

    Engaging in open source projects showcases your collaborative skills and commitment to the tech community. It demonstrates your passion for coding beyond academic or work requirements.

  2. Blogging/Vlogging about Tech:

    Sharing your insights, tutorials, or experiences through a blog or vlog demonstrates your communication skills and your ability to simplify complex concepts for others.

  3. Hackathons and Coding Competitions:

    Participation in hackathons or coding competitions illustrates your problem-solving abilities, creativity, and agility in developing solutions within limited time frames.

  4. Graphic Design or Digital Art:

    Proficiency in graphic design or digital art highlights your creativity and aesthetic sense, which are valuable in web development for designing visually appealing interfaces.

  5. Fitness and Well-being:

    Pursuing physical activities or hobbies related to fitness and well-being not only demonstrates a balanced lifestyle but also shows discipline and determination, qualities that are transferable to the dedication required in coding and development.
